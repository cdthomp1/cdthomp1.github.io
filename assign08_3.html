Athletics

Embry-Riddle Eagles

The Embry-Riddle Office of Sports Information is responsible for providing accurate and timely information to a variety of customers, including media, student-athletes, coaches and fans. ERAU Sports Information reports on and publicizes all 20 varsity sports in addition to upholding and improving the image of the Intercollegiate Athletics Department on Embry-Riddle's Daytona Beach campus and Embry-Riddle University in general.

The ERAU Sports Information Office writes the content for Embry-Riddle's Athletic website, http://erauathletics.com, arranging all interviews with student-athletes and coaches, compiling and archiving statistics, writing game stories, and producing media guides and publications for all sports.
